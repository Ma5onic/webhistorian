Your Identifier

Web Historian has created a unique identifier that allows you to combine your data from this web browser with other browsers where you have installed Web Historian. You may want to do this to see a more complete picture of your web browsing behavior across different browsers. If you have installed Web Historian before and now you want to combine your data from a previous installation with this one just replace the identifier below with your previously-generated identifier. You must upload your data from each installation and complete the survey, then choose the "View on Server" menu option to see your combined visualizations.
